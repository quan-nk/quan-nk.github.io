<h2 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-left has-text-color has-background" style="color:#ebf715;background-color:#1d4374">Systematic reviews - Meta-analysis</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p> Get started with <a href="http://www.prisma-statement.org/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">PRISMA Statement Reporting Guidelines</a>, and read original articles from Dr <a href="https://www.bmj.com/content/372/bmj.n71" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Matthew J Page</a> (BMJ, 2021), and originally Dr <a href="https://www.acpjournals.org/doi/full/10.7326/0003-4819-151-4-200908180-00135" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Moher D</a> (Ann Int Med, 2009)</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><a rel="noreferrer noopener" href="https://training.cochrane.org/handbook/current" target="_blank">Cochrane Handbook</a> for Systematic Reviews of Interventions, by Drs Julian Higgins, James Thomas et al.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><a rel="noreferrer noopener" href="https://jbi-global-wiki.refined.site/space/MANUAL" target="_blank">JBI Manual</a> for Evidence Synthesis, by Drs Aromataris E, Munn Z et al.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>University of York, <a rel="noreferrer noopener" href="https://www.york.ac.uk/crd/" target="_blank">Center for Reviews and Dissemination</a> (CRD), with a sharp guidance and accessible databases</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><a href="https://browse.welch.jhmi.edu/sr-methods" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Welch Medical Library</a>, SR method guides</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><a rel="noreferrer noopener" href="https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/book/10.1002/9780470743386" target="_blank">Introduction to Meta-analysis</a>, &nbsp;a clear and thorough introduction to meta-analysis by Dr Borenstein, one of my favorite authors in SR-MA </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><a href="https://www.meta-analysis-workshops.com/download/commonmistakes.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Common Mistakes in Meta-analysis and How to Avoid Them</a>, also by Dr Borenstein. A hardcopy is readily accessible on Amazon</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><a href="https://bookdown.org/MathiasHarrer/Doing_Meta_Analysis_in_R/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Doing Meta-analysis in R</a>, a book by Mathias Harrer x CRC Press</p>
