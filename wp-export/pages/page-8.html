I earned my MD from Hue University in 2021. I am passionate about improving health of my community and envisioning a world where people can live a healthy, happy life.

Since 2018 I has worked with the NGO Community Medicine (Y Hoc Cong Dong) to address the knowledge gap and provide primary care to underserved populations in Vietnam. Amidst the COVID-19 crisis, my team successfully secured a $130,000 grant "Healing Saigon," providing essential medical supplies like masks, PPEs, HFNCs, etc. to hospitals, directly aiding in patient care.

I have been a working group leader at ORC (Nagasaki University, Japan) where we conduct systematic reviews and meta-analyses to consolidate knowledge from published RCTs and provide evidence-based recommendations for health interventions.

I appreciate R&B, nonfiction & classics, hiking & ecotourism, and blogging.

Opinions are my own.
